Web development is a valuable skill in today's digital age.

Warning: This action is irreversible.

<strong> (Strong - Important):

Important: Make sure to save your changes.

<i> (Italic):

This course is optional.

<em> (Emphasis - Semantically italic):

Please review your answers carefully.

<ins> (Inserted/Underline):

This feature was added in version 2.0.

The old price was $99.99.

<mark> (Marked/Highlighted):

Make sure to attend the meeting at 10 AM.

<small> (Small text):

This text is for legal disclaimers and copyright.

<sub> (Subscript):

<sup> (Superscript):

CSS is used to style web pages.

Algorithm is a step-by-step method to solve a problem.

Albert Einstein once said, Imagination is more important than knowledge.

<blockquote> (Block Quote):

"The best way to predict the future is to invent it." — Alan Kay

  console.log("Hello, world!");

Access granted. Welcome back, Robert!
